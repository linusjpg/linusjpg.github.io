Anonymize Photographs

Do No Harm: Photographing Police Brutality Protests
This page is supposed to give people the necessary tools to easily erase identifiable information from a photograph to protect the author's as well as the depicted individuals' privacy. Exif data is removed automatically in order to prevent leaking extremely sensitive metadata like the location. All pixels underneath the blur each get scattered into a random direction making any information unintelligible while the blur maintains an optical sense for aestethic reasons (example).
The photographs will never leave your device.
All image processing is done locally on your end.
